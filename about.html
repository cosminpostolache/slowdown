Introduction

I'm Cosmin, a massage therapist looking to help people overcome health problems and stress.

My Journey

I started studying massage 7 years ago, as a way of becoming in tune with the world through the body I had majored with a BA in Philosophy and was looking to also explore ideas in other traditions. This is when I discovered Chi Nei Tsang and began to practice it on myself and friends. I was amazed by the results and decided to get certified as a practitioner. These days I offer my sessions either in the comfort of your own home or in my own welcoming practice space. Please go to the contact page if you want to book a meeting.
