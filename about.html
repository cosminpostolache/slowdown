About Me

I am a certified practitioner of Chi Nei Tsang, and have been practicing various forms of massage for the past seven years.

I am also currently studying acupuncture at X School.

My Journey

I started exploring the world of massage after majoring in Philosophy.

While exploring the ideas of various Eastern traditions, I came across Taoism. These teachings maintained that various problems of the mind and body can be overcome using touch and concentration. One such technique was Chi Nei Tsang, an abdominal massage which promotes energy flow in the whole body.

I started practicing this and other forms of massage and was immediately drawn by their effects. I felt more in tune with the world and my own body, my mood was elevated and my mind was clear. I decided to get certified as a practitioner and see where it goes.

I have continued on this path of study, practice and humility. These days I can offer various forms of therapy in the comfort of your own home.
