About Me

I am a certified practitioner of Chi Nei Tsang, and have been practicing various forms of massage for the past seven years.

I am also currently studying acupuncture at X School.

My Journey

I started studying massage as a way of becoming in tune with the world through the body

I had majored with a BA in Philosophy and was looking to also explore ideas in other traditions.

This is when I discovered Chi Nei Tsang and began to practice it on myself and friends.

This is an abdominal massage which promotes energy flow in the whole body.

I was amazed by the results and decided to get certified as a practitioner.

These days I offer my sessions either in the comfort of your own home.

Slow down, London!

The logo of my initiative is a comfy badger, and here's why:

In a quieter London, badgers lived in gentle balance with their environment.

As the city grew, that balance faded, and they slipped away.

Their absence reminds us of the cost that our frenetic activity sometimes exerts all around us.
