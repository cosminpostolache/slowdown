About Me

I have been practicing various forms of massage for the past seven years.

I am also currently studying acupuncture at the City College of Acupuncture.

My Journey

I started exploring the world of massage after majoring in philosophy. While exploring the ideas of various Eastern traditions, I came across Taoism.

These teachings maintained that various problems of the mind and body can be overcome using touch and concentration. One such technique was Chi Nei Tsang, an abdominal massage which promotes energy flow in the whole body.

I started practicing this and other forms of massage and was immediately drawn by their effects. I felt more in tune with the world and my own body, my mood was elevated and my mind was clear. I decided to get certified as a practitioner and see where it goes.

After many years of practice, I can confidently offer various forms of therapy in the comfort of any client's home.

Slow down, London!

The logo of my initiative is a comfy badger, and here's why:

In a quieter London, badgers lived in gentle balance with their environment.

As the city grew, that balance faded, and they slipped away.

Their absence reminds us of the cost that our frenetic activity sometimes exerts all around us.
